About jumpstart

Jumpstart is a free web template hand crafted with love by us to you. It is aimed at businesses who are looking for a professional, slick look which is easy to setup but also a breeze to customise.


Feature packed

Who says free templates shouldn't be awesome?


  • 3 styles to choose from (or roll your own)
  • Homepage slider
  • 6 page templates to choose from
  • Use of the 960gs css system
  • Google maps integration
  • Fully working contact form
  • Cross browser compatible

Fully customisable

What we have created here is just the tip of the iceberg. Using your favourite web technologies you can run wild and create the perfect web solution to meet your needs.


Make it your own

You are free to take this template and do what you like with it, it's all yours!
